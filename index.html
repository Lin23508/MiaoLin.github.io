I work on securing AI models for edge devices by advancing defense mechanisms against adversarial and backdoor threats. My research secures AI for practical deployments such as voice assistants and facial recognition, combining theoretical guarantees with empirical systems work to improve robustness and efficiency in real-world settings. Recent work proposes a certified poisoned-sample detector under dataset imbalance and an efficient randomized smoothing framework for robustness certification on resource-constrained devices.
